Madame.

All the country was ‘en plein vendange,’ she told us; wherever we went we could see the vintagers, and if we wished to make a ‘jolie petite course à pied_’ we could not do better than walk to the little village of St. Lambert. _En effet, she herself was propriétaire, and it would give her son great pleasure to show us his cuvier and all else that we might care to see.

‘And peasants?’ we said vaguely; ‘we want to talk to the peasants.’

Madame looked slightly bewildered.
